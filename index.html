About Me

My name is Olga Kolin and I am a soon-to-be business finance graduate of Dominican University of California. Throughout my 3 years at Dominican, I have harnessed knowledge from not only my classes, but also from my peers, community, experiences and internships; all of which have shaped my goals as I close out this chapter of my life.

My love for the business world is endless and I am excited to pursue a long-time dream of working in the financial district of San Francisco. In June of 2019, I will be starting at Merrill Lynch as a financial advisor trainee. I am excited to dive into this new unknown and challenge myself in hopes of further developing my knowledge and growth.

Most importantly, I am beginning a journey of helping people attain wellness and happiness.

Career

My mission is to utilize my developing career as a financial advisor to help people better understand how to manage their financial lives. With a respect for finance and a love for people and relationship building, I strive to educate people how to attain financial well-being. I also have passion for health and fitness. Thus, I can combine my passions with my career to become a wellness advisor and help people live better lives in a holistic way.

Vision

Alongside building a career, I plan on further developing my many hobbies which include living an active and healthy lifestyle, wholesome cooking and the outdoors.

Financial wellness is important but so is our overall wellness. Along with taking care of our budgets, I hope to inspire people to also nourish their minds and bodies.
